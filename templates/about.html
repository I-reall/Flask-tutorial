About Us

Sanny's Cake Shop is founded by Sanelisiwe Bikitsha, widely known as Sanny, in Cape Town. Our shop specializes in handcrafted cakes and pastries made from the finest ingredients. Each cake is a piece of art, crafted with care and designed to add that special touch to your celebrations.

At Sanny's, we believe every cake tells a story. Join us in our journey to make every occasion memorable with a perfect cake.
