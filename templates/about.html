About Us

Sanny's Cake Shop is not just a business; it's a passion brought to life by the vibrant and creative Sanelisiwe Bikitsha, fondly known as Sanny. Nestled in the heart of Cape Town, our shop began as a small home-based operation in 2015, when Sanny decided to turn her love for baking into a full-time pursuit.

Specializing in custom-made cakes, each creation by Sanny is more than just a dessert; it's a personal masterpiece, tailored to the tastes and dreams of our clients. Sanny's Cake Shop has become renowned for its exquisite designs, exceptional taste, and the ability to bring a sprinkle of joy to every occasion.

Our signature cakes include the Chocolate Delight, a rich and moist chocolate cake loved for its depth of flavor; the Vanilla Cloud, a light and fluffy cake that feels like biting into a sweet cloud; and the Berry Fusion Tart, a perfect blend of tart and sweetness, celebrated for its vibrant layers of fresh berries and smooth cream.

At Sanny's, we believe in sustainability and community. We source our ingredients from local suppliers and organic farms, ensuring that each cake not only tastes good but also supports our community and environment.

Join us on our journey to make each celebration memorable with a cake that tells a story, your story.
